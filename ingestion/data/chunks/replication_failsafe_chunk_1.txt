Considerations

For fail-safe and Snowpipe Streaming Classic, be aware of the following limitations:
Fail-safe doesn’t support tables that contain data ingested by Snowpipe Streaming Classic. For such tables, you can’t use fail-safe for recovery because fail-safe operations on that table will fail completely. For more information, see
Snowpipe Streaming limitations
